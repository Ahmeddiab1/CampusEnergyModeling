This is a simple MLE+ example with a small office building. 
You can control the setpoints of the rooms. The outputs are 
set to change be the output temperature and room temperature. 

Instructions: 
Take a Look at the MLE+ instructions included in the Library Folder.  

Features:
• Single E+ building in Simulink. Small Office Building with Temperature Setpoints (Heating and Cooling) as knobs.
